Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Kaju Bot collects, stores, processes, and protects information while providing Discord bot services, AI systems, multiplayer systems, economy systems, music features, and related services.



1. Information Collected

Kaju Bot may collect and process:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Discord Channel IDs
  • Command usage information
  • Economy balances and progression
  • Fishing inventories and assets
  • Level, XP, and achievement systems
  • Music session information
  • AI prompts and generated responses
  • Temporary voice processing data
  • Moderation or anti-abuse logs
  • Error logs and diagnostics
  • Configuration and settings data

2. Usage of Information

Collected information may be used to:

  • Provide gameplay and bot functionality
  • Save user progress and inventories
  • Operate AI systems and commands
  • Maintain multiplayer and event systems
  • Improve performance and stability
  • Detect abuse, exploits, or spam
  • Protect systems and users
  • Debug technical issues

3. AI & Voice Features

Certain features may temporarily process text prompts, interactions, or voice-related data to generate AI responses or provide voice-enabled functionality.

Voice or AI data is not intended for permanent storage unless required for security, moderation, debugging, or feature functionality.


4. Economy & Gameplay Systems

Kaju Bot stores gameplay-related data such as:

  • Fishing progress
  • Inventory items
  • Currencies and rewards
  • Cooldown systems
  • Achievements and unlockables
  • Boss raid participation
  • Player progression and rankings

5. Third-Party Services

Kaju Bot may use third-party services including:

  • Discord APIs
  • Cloudflare services
  • Hosting providers
  • Database systems
  • AI APIs
  • Voice processing systems
  • Analytics and monitoring systems

6. Data Storage

Data may be securely stored in databases or hosting systems necessary for operation of bot features and services.


7. Data Sharing

Kaju Bot does not intentionally sell, rent, or trade user information to third parties. Information may only be shared if required by law, security enforcement, or abuse prevention.


8. Security

Reasonable security measures are used to help protect stored information. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.


9. User Rights

Users may request removal of stored data through the official support server.


10. Abuse Prevention

Systems may automatically log suspicious, abusive, exploitative, or malicious activity to maintain service stability and security.


11. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be modified, updated, or changed at any time without prior notice.


12. Contact

For support, questions, or requests, contact the official Kaju support server.
